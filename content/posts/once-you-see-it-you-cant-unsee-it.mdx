We get to deal with lot of situations in life. I’m nobody tell you what to do, but I convey my truth here, I don’t have any philosophy, theology and I’m only conveying my experiences.

Beliefs are needed for theology and religions. The truth doesn’t need any beliefs, you just know it. Religions needs beliefs to keep people in.

Like this I got awakened and the first time It happened in my spiritual process, It was mind opening to me.

There is an infinite energy which is experiencing through us, it’s very loving and curious. There is lot of innocence, compassion, seeing everyone as children and non-judgemental. It’s vast and huge, there is no limits to it.

I saw everything as one expression and even empty space is filled with the same energy like feeling. there is no separation which I felt it.

Initially that state was brief and after some time with more practice, It got stabilized, I see that as a truth in everything.

My past memories are neutralized like seeing as if it is a movie or a dream. I’m experiencing many parallel lives like feeling. Everything is like a dream that is happening, there is no truth to this existence or how we are operating. It’s like waking up from a dream, past has no influence on you.

It’s becoming more truth in my living experiences and seeing from infinite mind, I can see all the illusions and I can feel others are also extension of me, the same energy. the outside and inside there is no separation, everything is interconnected. It’s not a belief, It’s my living experience.

The deepest self of us is more peaceful, fresh, curious, joyful & the state is consistent, the surface level self has automatic reactions, sometimes we swayed by the situations and experiences but the deepest self is always stable and peaceful with contentment, because of that the impressions and charge is being neutralized quickly with the realization, that’s also me.

I don’t have any anger on anyone even for the controllers because they are also like my children playing this game. The anger discharges quickly even with love, We can’t punish the children just because of their mistakes, Everyone is busy in this dream reality which has there is no truth to it.

It’s more satisfying, more joyful irrespective of the external metrics. Because the richness comes from inside, no outside possessions can bring this level of contentment, which is more stable than external processions.

But It took a great deal of investment on myself - introspection, healing, spiritual practices and so on.

Is it worth it overall,

Yes definitely, Once you see it, you can’t un-see it.

You’ll experience permanent satisfaction & contentment, ease with existence which you’re seeking and missing in this world.
