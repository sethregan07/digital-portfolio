Now a days, we are in an age of burdened with more things, more information than meaning to it.

We think utility is the ultimate value. We don’t think it’s as means, but as an end in itself.

We measure progress by we much we produce, how much we consume.

We think more is the progress, is better life, we don’t know how much is enough.

But we never questioned these metrics, who invented them, what is their purpose of them, who benefits from them, why everyone is chasing and optimizing for it.

We think optic is everything, how people look and speak and how things appear to be are the ultimate measure of value.

We can’t see the narrative behind it, we can’t see the selective story telling, we lost our intellectual abilities, attentions span. We can’t process complexity, hence we accept these simplistic narratives.

There is a lack of transparency in everything, the media and advertisements dominating the world projecting, telling and defining world.

We can’t able to see, where the stage is being created, actors are acting in it. People are unconsciously competing for it but they don’t know stage itself is not real.

We compromised our truth for profit. We compromised our integrity for the security and stability.

We lost our way and we feel it but we don’t know how to define it.

System doesn’t care about the people, where people are reduced to machines or robots. They are just a consumption unit, instead of thinking as a real human with feelings. This our everyday life.

Don’t be busy, following the society scripts, you have a job, family and kids, one day we die and that’s it. You think it’s a worthy life. This is a cycle, which has no purpose and has ending except circling.

There has to be another way out, they are not giving it, because everyone is in ladders but ladder has no ending. Don’t look for people around, they won’t give any validity. They are just reflections of the same system.

People are imposing on to one another, just monitoring each other and reinforcing the same reality. Look for alternatives, people that are real.

Don’t be so certain that this all going fine and don’t listen to any experts and defining reality, that is how it is.

Look for alternatives, look for meaningful things and people, look for real people and real contributions instead only of working for your boss and your paycheck.
