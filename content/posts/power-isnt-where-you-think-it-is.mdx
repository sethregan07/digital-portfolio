Most people think power sits with politicians, CEOs, or celebrities.

But that’s not where it starts.

Power begins with narrative.

You don’t need to control millions of people. You only need to influence the flow of information.

Because information moves in layers:

• A few sources create ideas • Channels amplify them • Audiences absorb them • Over time, repetition becomes “truth”

And once enough people believe something…

It feels like reality.

Not because it’s proven. But because it’s widely accepted.

This is how consensus forms.

Not always through facts— but through alignment.

The real leverage isn’t controlling people.

It’s shaping what people see, hear, and repeat.

So the better question isn’t: “What is true?”

It’s: “How did this become true to so many people?”

When you start asking that, you stop being part of the crowd—

and start seeing the system.
